Privacy Policy

At IntelliNote, accessible from IntelliNote.io, one of our main priorities is the privacy of you, our users.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in IntelliNote. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.


Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.


Information we collect

When registering with your Google account, the only information we use is your email address, which is immediately encrypted with SHA256 before entering our servers.

This means that even if we wanted to, we cannot identify individual usernames to any personal information. At no point is your unencrypted email stored.


Content ownership

On IntelliNote, since you make the content, it belongs to you. However, in order to provide a better user experience, we reserve the right to:

  1. Showcase your songs on any site content that we produce
  2. Track statistics of your content on our platform, such as visitor count

Cookies and Web Beacons

In order to provide a seamless experience for you, the Google Authentication API stores cookies on your computer once you login. This is primarily to avoid having to sign in every time you navigate to a different page on our site, but Google may use this data for their own marketing use as well, as per their Terms. Our app works just as well without signing in through this service if this is something you'd prefer to avoid.

We also use an independent analytics solution in order to better understand our users' needs and to optimize the experience. In order to do so, some anonymous information may be tracked during your visit (e.g. how much time you spend on which pages, which links you choose to click, etc.), which lets us prioritize working on features that would benefit your experience here. These analytics uses cookies and other technologies to collect data on our users' behavior and their devices, which includes:

  1. a device's IP address (processed during your session and stored in a de-identified form)
  2. device screen size
  3. device type (unique device identifiers)
  4. browser information
  5. geographic location (country only)
  6. and the preferred language used to display our website.

This information is stored on our behalf in a pseudonymized user profile, and is contractually forbidden to be sold.
