Privacy Policy

At IntelliNote, accessible from IntelliNote.io, one of our main priorities is the privacy of you, our users.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in IntelliNote. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.


Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.


Information we collect

When registering with your Google account, the only information we use is your email address, which is immediately encrypted with SHA256 before entering our servers.

This means that even if we wanted to, we cannot identify individual usernames to any personal information. At no point is your unencrypted email stored.


Content ownership

On IntelliNote, since you make the content, it belongs to you. However, in order to provide a better user experience, we reserve the right to:

  1. Showcase your songs on any site content that we produce
  2. Track statistics of your content on our platform, namely visitor count

Log Files

IntelliNote follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages. This information can never be linked to any information that is personally identifiable. We use this information for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.


Cookies and Web Beacons

In order to provide a seamless experience for you, the Google Authentication API stores cookies on your computer once you login. This is primarily to avoid having to sign in every time you navigate to a different page on our site, but Google may use this data for their own marketing use as well, as per their Terms. Our app works just as well without signing in through this service.

To learn more about cookies, this article provides general information on what to expect.


Advertising Partners Privacy Policies

IntelliNote does not show any advertisements, contextual or targeted, to our users. In the event that an advertising network is established, this section will be updated with the relevant information.


CCPA/GDPR Privacy Rights

Under the CCPA and GDPR data privacy and protection rights, our users have every right to request a number of freedoms with any personal data that we store about them.

Thankfully, we don't store or sell any personally identifiable data, so although any CCPA/GDPR requests are welcome, we'll have to reply empty-handed.
